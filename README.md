## About
This game was a submission to the Pirate Software Game Jam with the theme of "It's spreading." Play as pirate Blackbeard and spread your infamy making enemies flee in terror. Combo your way through hordes of enemies and buy upgrades in the shop to level up. Be wary, the more coins you have, the more they will weigh you down.

## Controls
WASD to move

S fast fall

W jump - Hold down the jump for higher jumping!

Jump multiple times underwater to swim

SPACE or LMB shoot

MELEE/PARRY BOMBS AND CANNONBALLS - RMB or E

1 - Flintlock - 1 ammo cost

2 - Blunderbuss - 3 ammo cost

3 - Cannon - 5 ammo cost

R - Die and reset level

F11 - Fullscreen

Includes limited controller support as well

## Upgrades
Rum - More rum, more lives!

Springleg - Jump twice as high

Wheelboots -  Move twice as fast

Jumping Beans - Unlocks double jump

Extra Barrel - Let's you shoot twice as fast

Loudener - Doubles your gun damage

Jolly Roger - Increases your chances of infamy triggering fear

Dread Pirate Bob's Sword - Makes parried bombs and cannonballs fly back with deadly force!
